eLearning Local Video

Geminate comes with a handy feature to upload any local video on eLearning slides instead of online video url of youtube, google drive or anyother 3rd party cloud public url.
So no need of any 3rd party websites intervention on your inhouse eLearning plateform.







Editions Supported

1. Community Version

2. Enterprise Version

Available Key Features

  • You can easily upload any local video in your attachment.
  • You can choose those attached local video inside your eLearning slides.
  • No need of any 3rd party websites (youtube, vimeo, google drive etc) intervention on your inhouse eLearning plateform.
